Hearts...

...is a chrome extension that utilizes Facebook to help you find out your

relationship status with people you chat! It will analyze the chat messages

exchanged between you and your friend to determine which one of you is more

attached to the other. This application will provide you with a scale that

shows by how much you or friend talks more enthusiastically than the other.

It will also give you an exact percentage for the difference between the

amount of interest you and your friend have for each other.
